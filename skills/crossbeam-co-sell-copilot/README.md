# Crossbeam Co-Sell Copilot

Win, unblock, or break into an account by bringing in the right partner — find who can help, work out the play, and draft the ask. A free, vendor-neutral skill for any team that connects the [Crossbeam](https://crossbeam.com) MCP.

## What it does

You name an account or a deal; the skill finds the partner best positioned to help, works out exactly how they can help, and drafts the outreach to make it happen:

- Finds which partner is best positioned on the account and how strong that position really is
- Works out the specific play — an intro to a stakeholder you're missing, intel on how the account buys, a backchannel reference, a voucher for urgency, coordinating a joint deal, or a marketplace co-sell
- Pinpoints the partner contact, and the partner rep to ask
- Suggests something to offer the partner back, so the ask is a trade, not a favor
- Drafts a short, rep-to-rep message, with a hand-off to your co-sell workspace where it fits

Works on a single account, a list, or a pipeline filter ("deals stuck in stage 3").

## What you need

- The **Crossbeam MCP** connected and authenticated (free to connect at [crossbeam.com](https://crossbeam.com))
- Optional: a CRM for pipeline filters and your own contact coverage, and Gmail/Outlook to draft into

## Try it

> "Who can help me win [account]?"
> "How do I break into [account]?"
> "I have 5 deals stuck in stage 3 — can you help me unlock them?"
> "Create urgency on [account] without discounting."

## What you get

For each account: the recommended partner, why they're positioned, the play to run, the warm-path contact, a reciprocal give, and a drafted rep-to-rep ask you review before anything goes out.

## Why this beats calling the MCP directly

The Crossbeam MCP gives you the data; this skill gives you the judgment and the safe next step:

- **It picks, and says why.** Not "here are ten partners" — it scores position strength and names the one to work, with the reason.
- **It finds the gap.** It crosses the partner's contacts against the ones you already have and names the stakeholder only the partner can reach.
- **It chooses the play.** Intro, intel, reference, voucher, coordinate, or marketplace — the right move for where the deal is.
- **It does the safe thing.** It addresses the *partner rep*, never the prospect; it uses the customer record on an expansion, not a stray duplicate; it says "not shared" rather than "no overlap." Those are the mistakes a raw query makes that cost you a relationship.

The MCP is the engine; this skill is the co-sell expertise that drives it — the same artifact every run, for every rep.

## Privacy and data

Read-only and draft-only. The skill only surfaces partner data your partners have already shared with you under your Crossbeam sharing rules, and it never sends anything without your review. Generic and vendor-neutral — it works for any company on the Crossbeam MCP.
