# Crossbeam Ecosystem Prospecting (EQL Finder)

Turn your partner ecosystem into a ranked prospecting list — who to work now, why each account, and the warm way in. A free, vendor-neutral skill for any team that connects the [Crossbeam](https://crossbeam.com) MCP.

## What it does

Most of your best prospects already touch your partner ecosystem — they're a partner's customer, or an account a partner is actively selling. Those are **ecosystem-qualified leads (EQLs)**, and they convert better than cold leads because the ecosystem behaviour is real buying signal you can't buy or scrape.

This skill finds them and makes them workable:

- Picks the partner pools whose customers best fit what you sell, then crosses your prospects against them to surface EQLs
- Scores and ranks them, so you work the strongest signal first
- Explains each one — which partner, customer or live deal, the partner AE
- Gives you a per-account angle and the warm-path contact
- Flags accounts a partner is mid-deal on, so you don't cold-prospect into their live opportunity
- Hands the list (and optional first-touch drafts) to your MAP or sequencer

## What you need

- The **Crossbeam MCP** connected and authenticated (free to connect at [crossbeam.com](https://crossbeam.com))
- Optional: a CRM for territory/segment resolution, and a MAP or sequencer to push the list to

## Try it

> "Find me EQLs in my enterprise territory."
> "Which of my prospects are customers of [partner]?"
> "Who should I prospect this week, and what's my angle?"

## What you get

A ranked list — one row per account with its EQL tier, the partner relationship driving it, a better-together angle, and the warm-path contact — ready to push to your sequencer. Optionally, drafted first touches that lead with the joint value.

## Why this beats calling the MCP directly

The Crossbeam MCP gives you the data primitives. This skill gives you the expertise to use them well — the part the raw tools leave to you:

- **It picks the right pool.** "Find me EQLs for our [product]" becomes an anchored query on the partners that fit — not an overlap dump sorted by popularity that floats megacorps to the top.
- **It ranks for your goal.** The tools return rows; the skill computes a composite EQL score (partner-is-customer beats partner-is-opp, weighted by reach, fit, and partner quality) and orders the list.
- **It explains and angles.** It turns "this account is a partner's customer" into *why to reach out now* and *how to open* — synthesis a data lookup can't produce.
- **It protects the partnership.** It won't point you at an account a partner is mid-deal on; it routes that to co-sell instead.

In short: the MCP is the engine; this skill is the ecosystem-led-growth playbook that drives it — the same expert workflow every run, for a rep who's never heard the term "EQL."

## Privacy and data

Read-only. The skill only surfaces partner data your partners have already shared with you under your Crossbeam sharing rules, and it never sends or pushes anything without your review. Generic and vendor-neutral — it works for any company on the Crossbeam MCP.
